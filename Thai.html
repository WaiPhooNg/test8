Basic History 1

Image source : Unspalsh.com

From the 800s through the 13th century, the Khmer Empire controlled Thailand for many years. Local Thai chiefs banded together in the 13th century and drove the Khmer Empire out of Thailand. In 1238, they founded the Thai Kingdom.The Thai Kingdom of Ayutthaya was founded in 1350. Rama Thibodi was the country's first king. King Thibodi established the Dharamshastra rule of law and proclaimed Theravada Buddhism the national religion.

Basic History 2

For about 400 years, the Kingdom of Ayutthaya governed the land. Burmese armies invaded and burnt the capital in 1767. The Chakri dynasty arose in 1782 in the city of Chao Phraya, which is now Bangkok. The Chakri dynasty's first ruler was Rama I. Thailand is still ruled by the Chakri family, and King Adulyadej has been in power since 1946. European explorers dubbed Thailand Siam and its people Siamese. During the colonization period, Thailand barely avoided being colonized by any European country.
